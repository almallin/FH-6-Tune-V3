Understeer
U1Entry Understeer
Won't turn in under braking
  • Front ARB Soften
  • Front spring Soften
  • Front rebound Soften
  • Brake bias More rear
  • Diff decel Reduce
  • Caster Increase
U2Mid-corner Understeer
Pushes wide at constant throttle
  • Front ARB Soften
  • Front tyre pressure Lower
  • Front camber More negative
  • Front downforce Increase
  • Front ride height Lower
U3Exit Understeer (AWD/FWD)
Front washes on power out of corner
  • Diff accel Reduce
  • Rear spring Stiffen
  • Rear toe-in Reduce
  • Rear downforce Reduce
  • Rear bump Stiffen
U4High-speed Understeer
Plows in fast sweepers only
  • Front downforce Increase
  • Front camber More negative
  • Front ride height Lower
  • Rear downforce Reduce
Oversteer
O1Entry Oversteer
Rear steps out on braking/lift-off
  • Diff decel Increase
  • Rear ARB Soften
  • Brake bias More front
  • Rear spring Soften
  • Front rebound Stiffen
O2Mid-corner Oversteer
Rear slides at constant throttle
  • Rear ARB Soften
  • Rear camber More negative
  • Rear downforce Increase
  • Rear ride height Lower
O3Power Oversteer
Rear breaks free on throttle (RWD classic)
  • Diff accel Reduce
  • Rear toe-in Increase
  • Rear spring Soften
  • Rear bump Soften
  • Rear downforce Increase
O4Snap Oversteer
Rear comes around suddenly without warning
  • Rear ARB Soften
  • Rear spring Soften
  • Rear rebound Soften
  • Rear bump Soften
  • Diff accel Reduce
Other Problems
B1Car on Two Wheels
Lifting inside wheel in corners (light cars)
  • ARBs Max/near max
  • Springs Stiffen both
  • Ride height Lower
  • Rebound Increase
  • Diff accel Reduce
B2Nose Dives Under Braking
  • Front bump Stiffen
  • Front spring Stiffen
  • Brake pressure Reduce
B3Bouncy / Floaty
  • Bump both Stiffen
  • Rebound both Stiffen
  • Springs Stiffen
B4Skitters Over Bumps
  • Bump Soften
  • Springs Soften
  • ARBs Soften
  • Ride height Raise
B5Bottoms Out
  • Ride height Raise
  • Springs Stiffen
  • Bump Stiffen
B6Wheelspin on Exit
  • Diff accel Reduce
  • Rear spring Soften
  • Rear bump Soften
  • Rear downforce Increase
B7Lazy in Chicanes
  • ARBs both Stiffen
  • Rebound both Stiffen
  • Springs Stiffen

Golden rules: Fix the end with the problem. Change one thing at a time. Diagnose by corner phase first. Small steps. Tweak springs before damping extremes. When in doubt, reset to baseline.
